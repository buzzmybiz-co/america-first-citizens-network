About Us

Learn more about our mission and history.

Our Mission

The America First Citizens Network is a 501(c)(4) non-profit organization established to empower the American people. We believe in preserving the constitutional foundations that made our country strong, prosperous, and free.

In an era where everyday citizens often feel forgotten by the powerful, we act as a unified voice to advance policies that defend our economy, protect our borders, and ensure our liberties remain uninfringed.

Who We Are

We are a coalition of passionate, patriotic citizens from all walks of life—parents, small business owners, veterans, and community leaders—united by the shared belief that America is an exceptional nation. Our leadership team consists of seasoned advocates and policy experts who know how to enact meaningful change at local, state, and federal levels.

How We Work

Through grassroots organization, educational campaigns, and digital advocacy, we ensure that the priorities of the American people take precedence. We mobilize our members to contact their representatives, participate in local government, and rally behind initiatives that put America first.
