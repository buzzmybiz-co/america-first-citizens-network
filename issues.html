Our Priorities

Advocating for policies that strengthen our nation.

Innovating Technology Securing Our Nation Championing Conservative Leaders

Innovating Technology

We champion minimal AI regulation, rapid data center and chip manufacturing expansion, and American dominance in the global AI race. America must lead the world in artificial intelligence, or we will fall dangerously behind our greatest strategic rival, Communist China. Overregulation and bureaucratic delays are already slowing our progress while China aggressively builds its own AI capabilities. We refuse to let red tape hand the future to Beijing.

That’s why we fight for lightning-fast construction of data centers, massive onshoring of advanced chip manufacturing, and policies that unleash American innovation instead of stifling it with endless rules. By removing unnecessary barriers, we can accelerate AI development, create high-paying American jobs, strengthen our national security, and ensure the United States — not China — sets the global standards for this transformative technology.

Securing Our Nation

A strong nation requires strong borders, a powerful military, and an unwavering commitment to law and order in our communities. Weak borders invite chaos, crime, and exploitation, while a hollowed-out military signals weakness to our adversaries. When lawlessness is tolerated in our streets and cities, American families suffer the most. True national strength begins with securing our sovereignty, rebuilding our armed forces, and restoring respect for the rule of law.

America First Citizens Network stands firm for policies that put American safety and security first. We support leaders who will finish the border wall, enforce our immigration laws without apology, modernize and fully fund our military to deter China and other threats, and back our police so they can keep our neighborhoods safe again. A strong nation isn’t built on open borders and soft-on-crime policies — it is built on strength, order, and unapologetic defense of the American people.

Championing Conservative Leaders

Training and supporting candidates to boldly confront threats to our culture, values, and Constitutional Republic. Radical ideologies, government overreach, and cultural Marxism are actively undermining the foundations of American life — attacking our families, rewriting our history, and eroding the God-given rights protected by our Constitution. We cannot afford timid leaders who compromise or retreat. America needs courageous fighters willing to stand up and defend what makes our nation exceptional.

That’s why America First Citizens Network identifies, trains, and backs principled candidates who will fearlessly push back against woke indoctrination in our schools, protect free speech from Big Tech censorship, safeguard religious liberty, and restore the Constitutional Republic our Founders entrusted to us. We are building a new generation of warriors ready to reclaim our culture, defend our values, and ensure the American way of life endures for future generations.
